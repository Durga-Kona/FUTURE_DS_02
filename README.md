# FUTURE_DS_02
# 📊 CUSTOMER RETENTION & CHURN ANALYSIS DASHBOARD

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze customer churn patterns and identify key factors affecting customer retention.  
This project helps businesses understand **why customers leave** and **how to improve retention strategies**.

---

## **🎯 Project Objective**

Customer churn is a major challenge for businesses.  
This dashboard analyzes customer data to:

✔ Identify customers likely to churn  
✔ Understand factors influencing churn  
✔ Provide insights to improve retention  
✔ Support data-driven business decisions  

---

## **🛠 Tools & Technologies Used**

- **Power BI**
- **Data Visualization**
- **Business Intelligence**
- **Data Analytics**

---

## **📈 Dashboard KPIs**

The dashboard provides key performance indicators:

- **Total Customers**
- **Churn Customers**
- **Average Monthly Charges**

These KPIs give a quick overview of customer retention performance.

---

## **📊 Dashboard Features**

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

### **1️⃣ Churn Distribution**
Shows percentage of customers who stayed vs churned.

### **2️⃣ Churn by Contract Type**
Analyzes how contract duration affects customer retention.

### **3️⃣ Customer Retention Trend**
Shows how customer tenure impacts churn probability.

### **4️⃣ Monthly Charges vs Churn**
Identifies relationship between pricing and customer churn.

### **5️⃣ Churn by Tenure Group**
Segments customers based on experience level:

- **0–12 months → New customers**
- **12–24 months → Medium-term customers**
- **24+ months → Loyal customers**

---

## **💡 Key Insights**

- Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show highest churn.
- Customers with **higher monthly charges** are more likely to churn.
- Customers staying longer than **12 months** show better retention.
- Customers in the **first year** show highest churn risk.
- **Long-term customers (24+ months)** are more loyal.
- Improving **early customer experience** can significantly reduce churn.
